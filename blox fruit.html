Blox Fruit

Blox Fruit is a roblox game made by Gamer robot or we can say The Legendary Red Head King.
It was the most popular game for a long period of time but was sonn overtook by some brainrot games.
In blox fruit as the name suggests there are different kinds of fruits and weapons with different power levels.
Gameplay and progression
Missions and combat: Players earn experience to level up by completing missions and defeating enemies.
Magical fruits: These are special items that grant unique abilities and are fundamental to gameplay.
Combat and strategy: The game involves mastering the use of different fruits and improving equipment for different combat styles.
Fruit types and rarities
Fruit types: Fruits are categorized into three types: Paramecia, Zoan, and Logia.
Rarities: Fruits have different rarities, from Common to Mythical, which affects their cost and spawn rate.
Common: The cheapest and most likely to spawn.
Uncommon, Rare, Legendary, Mythical: Progressively more expensive and less likely to spawn.
